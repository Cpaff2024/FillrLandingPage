The Spark Behind Fillr

The idea for Fillr began with a common frustration: being on the go with a reusable water bottle, yet struggling to find convenient, reliable places to refill. The unnecessary cycle of purchasing single-use plastic, despite best intentions, was the catalyst. Fillr was conceived to solve this.

It soon became clear, however, that the potential extended far beyond water. From coffee cups to lunch containers, any item that can be 'refilled' represents an opportunity to reduce waste. Fillr's mission expanded: to create a platform where these eco-friendly choices are easily discovered, shared, and encouraged across our community.
